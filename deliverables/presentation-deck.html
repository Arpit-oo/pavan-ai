04
differentiators

what nobody else builds

killer feature

"what if we ban trucks?"

counterfactual intervention simulator with 5 policy types. empirical reduction factors from published delhi studies. before/after aqi per station.

unique

compound risk scoring

fuses aqi + wind stagnation + population density + vulnerable sites (schools, hospitals) + forecast trend into single 0-100 risk score per zone.

4-channel delivery

web dashboard · telegram bot (@PavanETbot) · email alerts (branded html) · whatsapp messages. health advisories in hindi, english, tamil, bengali.

grap auto-compliance

detects grap stages i-iv automatically. generates full compliance reports with executive summary, enforcement recommendations, and source attribution percentages.
