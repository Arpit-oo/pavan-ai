differentiators

killer feature

"what if we ban trucks?"

counterfactual intervention simulator with 5 policy types. empirical reduction factors from published delhi studies. before/after aqi per station. source-attribution-weighted.

compound risk scoring

fuses aqi + wind stagnation + population density + vulnerable sites (schools, hospitals) + forecast trend into single 0-100 risk score. not just "how bad" — "how dangerous for people right now."
