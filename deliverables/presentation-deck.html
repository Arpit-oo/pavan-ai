04
differentiators

what nobody else builds

killer feature

"what if we ban trucks?"

counterfactual simulation — 5 intervention types with empirical reduction factors from published studies. before/after aqi per station.

compound risk scoring

fuses aqi + wind stagnation + population density + vulnerable sites (schools, hospitals) + forecast trend → single 0-100 risk score per zone.

4-channel delivery

web dashboard · telegram bot (@PavanETbot) · email alerts (branded html via gmail) · whatsapp. health advisories in hindi, english, tamil, bengali.

grap auto-compliance

detects grap stages i-iv automatically. generates full compliance reports with executive summary, enforcement recs, and source attribution %.
