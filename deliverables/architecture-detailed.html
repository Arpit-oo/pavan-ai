ml pipeline detail

models + validation

XGBoost AQI forecast

200 estimators · max_depth 6 · 12 features
RMSE: 11.74 vs baseline 83.65 (86% gain)

pasquill-gifford A-F stability classes
predicts concentration grid from worst sources

inverse distance weighting → continuous AQI surface

compound risk scoring

AQI 35% + weather 20% + population 15%
+ vulnerable sites 15% + trend 15% → 0-100 score

wind + land use + time-of-day + PM ratio
