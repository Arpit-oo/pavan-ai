04
pavan.
ml pipeline detail

models + validation

XGBoost AQI forecast

200 estimators · max_depth 6 · lr 0.1
12 features: hour, day, month, temp, humidity, wind speed, wind dir (sin/cos), prev AQI (1h/6h/24h), weekend
RMSE: 11.74 vs baseline 83.65 (86% gain)

gaussian plume dispersion

pasquill-gifford stability classes A-F
based on wind speed, solar radiation, cloud cover
predicts concentration grid from worst sources

IDW interpolation

inverse distance weighting from sparse stations
generates continuous AQI surface for heatmap

compound risk scoring

5 factors weighted:
AQI (35%) + weather/stagnation (20%) + population density (15%) + vulnerable sites (15%) + forecast trend (15%)
output: single 0-100 risk score per zone

source attribution

wind direction → upwind source identification
land use profile per zone
time-of-day patterns + PM ratio
MODIS fire boost (2.5x crop, 1.5x industrial)

intervention simulator

source-attribution-weighted reduction factors
empirical from NGT 2023, GRAP 2024, SAFAR
per-station before/after AQI computation
