## SCRIPT (read this while screen recording)

### 0:00–0:15 — Landing Page
> "This is Pavan — an AI-powered air quality intelligence platform for India. We monitor 105 stations across 57 cities — every state capital covered. Let me show you what it does."

**Action:** Show landing page briefly. Scroll past stats. Click "explore dashboard."

---

### 0:15–0:35 — Dashboard
> "Here's the main dashboard. You can see live AQI data for Delhi — 18 stations with color-coded readings. The map shows pollution levels with graduated circles. Worst station right now is Wazirpur at 275."

**Action:** Point at the AQI tiles (190, PM2.5 101, worst 275, best 108). Show the map with station dots.

> "Below the map, we have our XGBoost forecast. I can switch between cities..."

**Action:** Click "Mumbai" pill under the forecast chart. Chart changes. Click "Lucknow" — chart shows higher AQI.

> "Each city has its own forecast based on historical patterns, weather, and seasonal data."

---

### 0:35–0:55 — Intervention Simulator
> "This is our killer feature — the intervention simulator. What happens if we ban trucks in Delhi?"

**Action:** Click "Simulator" in navbar. Click "Truck Ban" card.

> "We see AQI drops by about 8 points. The system uses empirical reduction factors from published Delhi pollution studies. We can compare all interventions..."

**Action:** Click "Compare All Interventions" button. Show the ranking.

> "Burning ban has the highest impact at 14.6 AQI reduction. These are evidence-based numbers."

---

### 0:55–1:15 — GRAP Compliance + Agents
> "For regulatory compliance, Pavan auto-detects GRAP stages and generates compliance reports."

**Action:** Click "GRAP" in navbar. Show the 4 stages. Click "Generate Compliance Report."

> "The report includes executive summary, enforcement recommendations, and source attribution — all auto-generated by our 6-agent AI pipeline."

**Action:** Quick click to "Agents" tab. Click "Run Full Analysis." Show agents working.

> "6 specialized agents coordinate in 3 phases — sensor, weather, anomaly detection, source attribution, and enforcement recommendations."

---

### 1:15–1:35 — Alerts + Chatbot
> "Citizens get alerts in 4 languages — English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali. We deliver via Telegram, email, and WhatsApp."

**Action:** Click "Alerts" in navbar. Show the 3 notification channel cards. Show WhatsApp preview.

> "And we have an AI chatbot powered by GPT-4o..."

**Action:** Click the blue chat bubble. Type "why is delhi so polluted?" Show the AI response.

> "It answers with real data from our agent pipeline."

---

### 1:35–1:50 — City Comparison
> "Finally, we can compare air quality across all 57 cities."

**Action:** Click "Compare" in navbar. Show the ranking. Click different cities to add them.

> "South Indian cities are consistently cleaner. North India — especially the Indo-Gangetic plain — has the worst air quality. This data helps policymakers prioritize."

---

### 1:50–2:00 — Close
> "Pavan. 105 stations. 57 cities. 6 AI agents. Real data, real intelligence, for every Indian city. Thank you."

**Action:** Show landing page or the "try pavan now" CTA at the bottom.

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Use Chrome, full screen (F11)**
2. **Open pavan-aqi.vercel.app** before recording
3. **Use OBS or Windows Game Bar** (Win+G) to record
4. **Resolution:** 1920x1080 preferred
5. **Mouse movements:** Slow and deliberate, don't rush
6. **Voice:** Clear, confident, slightly fast pace (2 min is tight)
7. **Pre-click everything once** before recording so pages are cached and load fast
8. **Telegram bot (optional):** If time permits, show a quick /delhi command in Telegram app on phone split-screen
